Transforming Furikake Gohan


Description

Transforming Furikake Gohan is a dish made by Sōma Yukihira for Erina Nakiri during his enrollment test at Tōtsuki Culinary Academy. It is Restaurant Yukihira's 8th dish on the secret menu.

Ingredients

  • Chicken Wings
  • Bonito Stock
  • Ginger
  • Onion
  • Sake
  • Mirin
  • Soy sauce
  • Eggs

Recipe

  1. Heat sesame oil in frying pan and sauté chicken wings until golden brown on both sides.
  2. Pour broth into a container to cool. Once cooled, place in refrigerator to chill and harden. Debone chicken wings and cut into strips.
  3. Pour aspic, egg curds, and chicken strips over hot rice. Sprinkle with diced spring onion and enjoy!

Osumatsu!
