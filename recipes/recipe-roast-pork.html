Ingredients

  • White Potato
  • Eringi mushrooms
  • White Onion
  • Butter
  • Rosemary
  • Bacon
  • Soy sauce
  • Parsley

Recipe

  1. Peel the Potatoes
  2. Slice the Potatoes
  3. Chop the Mushrooms and Onions into Equally-sized Brunoise (small 5-6cm cubes)
  4. Steam the Potatoes
  5. Add the Garnish
  6. Serve
